About Us

Project involves

Data Collection

Data collection is the process of gathering and measuring information on variables of interest in an established systematic fashion. It enables us to answer stated research questions, test hypotheses, evaluate outcomes, and generate meaningful insights from the data.

Data Preparation

Data preparation is the process of cleaning, transforming, and organizing data to make it suitable for analysis. It is an important step in the data analysis process, as the quality of the data used can have a significant impact on the accuracy and reliability of the results.

Data Visualization

Data visualization is the process of creating graphical representations of data to help people understand and explore information. The goal of data visualization is to make complex data sets more accessible, intuitive, and easier to interpret. By using visual elements such as charts, graphs, and maps, data visualizations help quickly identify patterns, trends, and outliers in the data.
